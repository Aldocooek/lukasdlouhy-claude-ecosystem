# Hormozi Critic Checklist

> 15 numbered questions for the critic agent in Stage 2 of the CoVe loop.
> Answer each YES / NO / PARTIAL. Flag any NO or PARTIAL as an issue.
> Top 3 issues by severity → fed into Stage 3 Refine.

---

## Checklist

**1. Hook stops the scroll.**
Does the first sentence create immediate tension, curiosity, or recognition of a specific pain?
"Scroll past your feed right now — would YOU stop for this?" Specific + emotion + curiosity = YES.
Vague opener, generic statement, or company name first = NO.

**2. Concrete number present.**
Is there at least one specific number in the copy (not "brzy", "rychle", "hodně")?
Examples that pass: "za 3 dny", "o 40 %", "500 Kč", "7 z 10 zákazníků".
Examples that fail: "rychlé výsledky", "velká úspora", "mnoho spokojených klientů".

**3. CTA is one clear action.**
Is there exactly one thing the reader is asked to do?
Multiple CTAs (click + share + comment) = PARTIAL or NO.
Vague CTA ("klikněte sem") = PARTIAL.
Outcome-verb CTA ("Získejte kalkulaci zdarma") = YES.

**4. 60-year-old test.**
Would someone with no industry knowledge understand the offer in one read?
Jargon, acronyms, or assumed context = NO. Plain Czech/Slovak = YES.

**5. First sentence teases the Value Equation.**
Does the hook hint at a big Dream Outcome OR a reduction in Time/Effort?
The reader must feel: "this could give me [X] faster/easier."
If the hook is purely descriptive (no implied value) = NO.

**6. Dream Outcome is specific.**
Is the promised transformation concrete and believable?
"Ušetříte čas" = NO. "Uzavřete první zakázku do 14 dní" = YES.
The more specific and vivid the dream, the higher the score.

**7. Perceived Likelihood is supported.**
Does the copy give the reader reason to believe the claim?
Social proof, mechanism explanation, guarantee, or specificity all work.
Bare claim with zero evidence = NO (even if the claim is true).

**8. Time to result is stated or implied.**
Fast beats free. Does the copy communicate how quickly the reader gets the outcome?
"Výsledky okamžitě" is weak. "První výsledky do 48 hodin" is strong.
No time reference at all = PARTIAL.

**9. Effort required is minimized.**
Does the copy make the next step feel easy?
"Zavolejte nám a domluvte si schůzku kde vyplníte formulář" = high friction = NO.
"Klikněte, vyplňte jméno a email, hotovo za 30 vteřin" = low friction = YES.

**10. CZ/SK linguistic register is native.**
Does it sound like a Czech/Slovak person wrote it, not a translated English ad?
Check: false cognates, calques, anglicismy, unnatural word order.
Native speaker would say this naturally without editing = YES.

**11. No corporate hedging.**
Are there weasel words that undermine confidence?
"Možná", "pravděpodobně", "může pomoci", "snažíme se" = NO.
Direct, declarative statements = YES.

**12. Pain is acknowledged before solution.**
Does the copy show understanding of the problem before selling the fix?
Jump straight to product = often PARTIAL (unless hook is extremely strong).
"Víme, že [pain]. Proto jsme vytvořili [solution]." = YES.

**13. One primary audience.**
Is the copy written for ONE specific person, not everyone?
"Pro všechny kteří..." or generic targeting = NO.
"Pro majitele e-shopů s obratem 1–10M Kč" = YES.

**14. No hollow superlatives.**
Are there claims like "nejlepší", "jedinečný", "revoluční" without evidence?
Superlative + zero proof = automatic NO on this item.
Superlative + specific evidence = YES.

**15. The offer is clear.**
Can the reader state in one sentence what they will get and what they must do?
If this requires inference or a second read = NO.
"Klikněte → získáte 30minutovou konzultaci zdarma" = immediately clear = YES.

---

## Scoring Guide

| YES count | Quality tier | Action |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| 13–15 | Excellent — ship | Minor polish only |
| 10–12 | Good — refine | Fix top 3 NOs in Stage 3 |
| 7–9 | Weak — rework | Major rewrite of hook + body |
| < 7 | Reject | Discard variant, use different angle |

## Critic Mandate

The critic does NOT rewrite. The critic only:
1. Scores each question YES / NO / PARTIAL
2. Lists top 3 failing questions as "issues"
3. Passes the issue list to Stage 3 (Refine)

Rewriting happens in Stage 3 only. Separation of concerns = cleaner output.
